About Joelle's Lounge

Fine dining rooted in community, craft, and a genuine love of great food.

Who We Are

A passion for food,
a love for people

Joelle's Lounge was born from a simple belief: that a great meal is about more than just the food on your plate. Founded by Joelle Martin, the restaurant grew out of years of traveling, cooking, and a deep appreciation for the way a shared table can bring people together.

From day one, we've been committed to sourcing the finest local and seasonal ingredients, partnering with nearby farms and artisan producers who share our values.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, enjoying a quiet dinner for two, or gathering with friends, Joelle's Lounge is your place. We're here to make every visit feel like coming home.
